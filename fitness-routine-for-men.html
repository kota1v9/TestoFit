Complete 7-Day Fitness Routine for Men

This full-week workout plan focuses on maximizing testosterone production, muscle growth, and overall fitness. Each day targets specific muscle groups using proven compound and accessory exercises, combined with smart recovery strategies. Use quality equipment to enhance performance and stay safe.

Day 1: Legs & Core

Exercises:

  • Barbell Squats (Quads, Glutes, Hamstrings, Core) – 4 sets of 8–10 reps
  • Romanian Deadlifts (Hamstrings, Glutes, Lower Back) – 3 sets of 8–10 reps
  • Walking Lunges (Quads, Glutes) – 3 sets of 12 steps each leg
  • Plank (Core) – 3 sets of 30–60 seconds
  • Leg Raises (Lower Abs) – 3 sets of 15 reps

Tip: Squats are the foundation for testosterone-boosting workouts. Use a solid squat rack for safety and proper form.

Day 2: Chest & Triceps

Exercises:

  • Barbell Bench Press (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps) – 4 sets of 8–10 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press (Upper Chest, Shoulders) – 3 sets of 8–12 reps
  • Dips (Triceps, Chest) – 3 sets of 10–15 reps
  • Tricep Pushdowns (Triceps) – 3 sets of 12–15 reps

Tip: Dumbbells offer a greater range of motion and balance. Try the CAP Barbell Hex Dumbbells for versatility.

Day 3: Back & Biceps

Exercises:

  • Deadlifts (Lower Back, Glutes, Hamstrings) – 4 sets of 6–8 reps
  • Pull-Ups (Lats, Biceps) – 4 sets of max reps
  • Bent Over Rows (Back, Rear Delts) – 3 sets of 8–10 reps
  • Bicep Curls (Biceps) – 3 sets of 12 reps

Tip: Maintain strict form on deadlifts to avoid injury. Use quality weight plates like the Fitvids Olympic Plates for smooth training.

Day 4: Active Recovery & Mobility

Focus on light cardio, stretching, foam rolling, or yoga to improve flexibility and reduce muscle soreness. This helps maintain hormonal balance and supports muscle repair.

Day 5: Shoulders & Abs

Exercises:

  • Overhead Barbell Press (Shoulders, Traps) – 4 sets of 8–10 reps
  • Lateral Raises (Side Delts) – 3 sets of 12–15 reps
  • Face Pulls (Rear Delts, Upper Back) – 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Russian Twists (Obliques) – 3 sets of 30 twists
  • Hanging Leg Raises (Abs) – 3 sets of 15 reps

Day 6: Full Body Strength

Exercises:

  • Front Squats (Quads, Core) – 4 sets of 8 reps
  • Weighted Pull-Ups (Back, Biceps) – 4 sets of 6–8 reps
  • Dumbbell Bench Press (Chest, Triceps) – 4 sets of 8–10 reps
  • Romanian Deadlifts (Hamstrings, Glutes) – 3 sets of 10 reps

Tip: Use an adjustable bench for dumbbell exercises and incline variations.

Day 7: Rest & Recovery

Complete rest or very light activity like walking or stretching. Focus on quality sleep (7–9 hours) and nutrition to support hormone balance and muscle growth.

General Tips for Maximizing Your Routine

  • Progressive Overload: Increase weight or reps slightly each week to continue muscle growth.
  • Nutrition: Eat enough protein (0.7–1g per lb bodyweight), healthy fats, and complex carbs.
  • Hydration: Stay well-hydrated to improve performance and recovery.
  • Sleep: Deep sleep helps regulate testosterone and muscle repair.
  • Consistency: Stick to your workout days and track progress for best results.
  • Form First: Never sacrifice technique for heavier weights to avoid injury.

Equip yourself with quality gear and stay disciplined — this plan will help you build muscle, boost testosterone naturally, and feel stronger every day.
